- **Bullets do not get `usedSkill`.** `evidencedBy` already supplies the skill→bullet
  edge; authoring both ends doubles the edges and answers no new query. Each deleted
  Project's `usedSkill` rolled up to its Position instead.
- **`rg:deliveredDuring` kept at zero instances.** `bulletOf` asserts the opposite
  relation, so it is not a substitute.
- **Verizon Neo4j claimed** (owner call). `Graph Databases.md`'s "property-graph engines
  are NOT covered" paragraph replaced with a narrower statement. Owner-reported with no
  CV/LinkedIn corroboration; the bullet records that.
- **Education dated by span, not `rg:issued`.** LinkedIn carries year ranges only; a
  required `issued` forces every note to invent a conferral month.
- **`rg:certifies` opened to Education.** Decision 3 always meant degrees, but the
  property was modeled for Certification alone — Master Data Management had no legal
  evidence path. I initially misread this as an unearned claim and demoted the skill;
  the user corrected me and the demotion was reversed.
- **EXIN badges deliberately not wired.** `app.exeed.pro` serves a TLS cert that expired
  2026-01-03, then 503s after ~30s. A verification link that trips a security warning and
  hangs is worse than none.
- **Employer name redacted forward, not rewritten out** (owner call): the name stays in
  `ffb3aec`'s history rather than rewriting 18 unpushed commits.
