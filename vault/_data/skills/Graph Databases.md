# Graph Databases

RDF triplestores specifically: GraphDB as the ground-truth store in
[[Virtual Knowledge Graph Connectivity]], plus the RDF retrievers in
[[Biomedical GraphRAG Bench]]. Property-graph engines (Neo4j, Cypher) are NOT
covered — that is M7 work and, until then, a genuine gap that applications
demanding Cypher should surface rather than paper over.
